Our Story

Butter & Bloom Bakery was founded with a simple belief: cakes should be just as beautiful as they are delicious. What started as a small passion for baking and design has grown into a bakery that celebrates creativity, craftsmanship, and connection.

Every cake is made from scratch using high-quality ingredients, thoughtful flavors, and a lot of love. From classic signature cakes to custom designs for life's biggest moments, we believe every celebration deserves something special.

What Makes Us Different

  • Seasonal flavors inspired by the moment
  • Custom designs tailored to your vision
